## Non-Negotiable Constraints

- Primary identity remains AI Systems Engineer.
- Secondary identity remains Product Engineer.
- Featured projects remain Mahoraga, Hybrid GenAI Transaction Categorizer, and The Loop.
- Do not invent missing evidence.
- Do not use fake screenshots, fake dashboards, fake metrics, fake demos, or placeholder public links.
- Do not redesign the portfolio unless a critical contradiction is discovered.
- Static content comes before motion.
- Motion is progressive enhancement, not the content.

## Evidence Status Meanings

- Known: link or artifact was provided in planning context but not checked in this repo session.
- Verified: link or artifact was checked and confirmed usable.
- Missing: explicitly absent or not provided anywhere.
- Needed: must be created.
- Not applicable: not relevant for this project.

Known does not mean safe to publish. Verify before public CTAs are implemented.
